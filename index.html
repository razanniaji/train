ABOUT US

OUR VISION

Cotton Fashion S.A. is a knitwear manufacturer with the aim of becoming the most important production source for fashion within the South East region of Europe.

OUR MISSION

We aim to provide customer satisfaction in terms of quality, innovation, flexibility, price and business ethics, providing equal importance to all of these aspects of enterpreneurship.

Cotton Fashion was created when major players of the Greek textile business “blended” and formed one powerful clothing manufacturer. The company is a vibrant and dynamic organization where state-of-the art technology is standard in business practice and communications. A highly skilled and enthusiastic workforce strives for nothing short of excellence in production, quality and service. One of the most critical factors of our success is complete verticalization, either in-house or via external subcontractors.
